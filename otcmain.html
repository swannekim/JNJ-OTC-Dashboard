Monthly Dashboard of OTC SKU

Hovering data "YYYYMM" contains date(1), due to Python datetime System. Please Ignore the Date and Only Check Year and Month.

1. SKU : Click once, and Delete SKU. Click again, and Restore SKU. Double-click and leave only one selected SKU.

2. Target : Click buttons to check each Brand's Target Distribution Rate.

3. Timeline : Drag and Drop for a focused view of the graph. Recommended for setting timeline. Autoscale button helps returning to the original view.

4. Download : Click download the plot as png file button, and download the view at the moment.

5. Calculation : Unless SKU was not placed in pharmacy throughout the month, the SKU is assumed as being placed. Denominator(=Total number of Pharmacy) might differ each month, due to store check.

Weekly Dashboard of OTC SKU

1. Visit Week : Week 1 starts from 2021-Jan. Based on ISO week date system.

2. Calculation : Unless SKU was not placed in pharmacy throughout the week, the SKU is assumed as being placed. Denominator(=Total number of Pharmacy) might differ each week, due to store check.
